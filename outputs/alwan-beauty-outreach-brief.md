# Alwan Beauty Salon Outreach Brief

## Public Presence Snapshot

Public directory results suggest Alwan Beauty Saloon is a ladies beauty salon in Abu Dhabi, UAE, around Al Mansouri Building / Najda Street / Al Danah. The presence appears directory-led rather than brand-led: business name, address, phone, and hours are visible, but there does not appear to be a strong owned website experience that presents services, portfolio, offers, booking, and trust signals in one polished place.

## Key Opportunities

- Build an owned website instead of relying only on directory listings.
- Present the salon as elegant, modern, and trustworthy before a customer calls.
- Structure services into clear categories: hair, nails, makeup, facials, grooming, bridal/events, and packages.
- Add premium visuals and a gallery/portfolio area so customers can quickly feel the salon’s quality.
- Make WhatsApp the primary conversion path for mobile users.
- Add FAQs, location, hours, and appointment guidance to reduce repetitive questions.
- Use packages/offers to make the salon easier to compare and easier to book.

## WhatsApp Automation Flow

Main menu:
- View services
- Ask for prices
- Book appointment
- Current offers
- Location and hours
- Bridal/event booking
- Speak with a human

Booking flow:
1. Ask customer for preferred service.
2. Ask preferred date and time.
3. Ask whether the visit is for daily beauty, event, or bridal.
4. Ask for customer name.
5. Confirm phone number.
6. Send summary to salon staff.
7. Staff confirms appointment manually.

Price/quote flow:
1. Ask service category.
2. Ask for details such as hair length, nail style, facial type, event date, or inspiration image.
3. Send estimated range or tell customer staff will reply with a custom quote.

Bridal/event flow:
1. Ask event date.
2. Ask services needed.
3. Ask number of people.
4. Ask location preference if home/event service is available.
5. Request inspiration images.
6. Hand off to staff for custom quote.
